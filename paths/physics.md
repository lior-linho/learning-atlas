# Physics Learning Path

## Goal

Build a university-level foundation in physics, from classical mechanics to quantum mechanics and statistical physics.

## Why This Matters

Physics provides the conceptual and mathematical basis for understanding natural systems, from laboratory-scale phenomena to astrophysical structures.
