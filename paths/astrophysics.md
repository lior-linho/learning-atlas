# Astrophysics Learning Path

## Goal

Build a foundation for computational and observational astrophysics, with long-term preparation for research in galactic dynamics, cosmology, and high-energy astrophysics.

## Why This Matters

Astrophysics connects physics, mathematics, computation, and observational data to study stars, galaxies, black holes, and the universe.
