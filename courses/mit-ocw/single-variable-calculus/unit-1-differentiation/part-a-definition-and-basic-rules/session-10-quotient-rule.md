# Session 10: Quotient Rule

## Goal

Learn and apply the quotient rule for differentiating ratios of functions.

## Main Formula

For \( y = \frac{u(x)}{v(x)} \),

\[
y' = \frac{u'v - uv'}{v^2}
\]

## Core Ideas

- Differentiate the numerator and keep the denominator.
- Subtract the numerator times the derivative of the denominator.
- Square the denominator.
