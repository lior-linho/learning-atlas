# Session 9: Product Rule

## Goal

Learn and apply the product rule for differentiating products of functions.

## Main Formula

For \( y = u(x)v(x) \),

\[
y' = u'v + uv'
\]

## Core Ideas

- Differentiate the first factor and keep the second.
- Keep the first factor and differentiate the second.
- Add both parts.
