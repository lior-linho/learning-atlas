# Session 11: Chain Rule

## Goal

Learn and apply the chain rule for differentiating composite functions.

## Main Formula

For \( y = f(g(x)) \),

\[
y' = f'(g(x))g'(x)
\]

## Core Ideas

- Differentiate the outside function.
- Keep the inside function unchanged.
- Multiply by the derivative of the inside function.
