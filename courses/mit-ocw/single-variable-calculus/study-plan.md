# Study Plan

## Stage 1: Differentiation

Goal: understand derivatives as rates of change, slopes, and local linear behavior.

Topics:

- Limits
- Definition of derivative
- Basic derivative rules
- Chain rule
- Product rule
- Quotient rule
- Derivatives of trigonometric functions
- Implicit differentiation

Outputs:

- derivative rule notes
- handwritten practice
- selected exercises
- mistake review

## Stage 2: Applications of Differentiation

Goal: use derivatives to analyze functions and solve applied problems.

Topics:

- Related rates
- Linear approximation
- Optimization
- Curve sketching
- Mean value theorem
- L'Hospital's rule

Outputs:

- problem-set attempts
- application notes
- exam-style review

## Stage 3: Definite Integral and Applications

Goal: understand integration as accumulation and area.

Topics:

- Riemann sums
- Fundamental theorem of calculus
- Area
- Average value
- Volumes
- Applications

Outputs:

- concept notes
- selected exercises
- formula summary

## Stage 4: Techniques of Integration

Goal: learn practical integration methods.

Topics:

- Substitution
- Integration by parts
- Trigonometric integrals
- Partial fractions
- Improper integrals

Outputs:

- method notes
- practice sets
- mistake review

## Stage 5: Infinite Series

Goal: understand convergence, power series, and approximation.

Topics:

- Sequences
- Series
- Convergence tests
- Taylor series
- Power series

Outputs:

- convergence test map
- Taylor series notes
- selected exercises

## Stage 6: Final Review

Goal: consolidate the course into exam-ready knowledge.

Outputs:

- formula sheet
- mistake list
- final review notes
- selected exam attempts
